# Phase 6: Scale, Automation & Finance

## Goal
Scale TokBiz with automation, richer financial reporting, and advanced business workflows for larger MSMEs and small enterprises.

## Scope
- Automated recurring expenses and bill payments
- Profit and loss reporting, cash flow forecasting, and financial dashboards
- Supplier purchase orders and procurement approvals
- Advanced inventory costing methods (FIFO/LIFO)
- Role-based access control for managers, accountants, and operators
- Audit logging and compliance reporting

## Deliverables
- Recurring transaction scheduler and approval flows
- Extended reporting endpoints for P&L, cash flow, and supplier performance
- Backend role and permission system
- Audit trail capture for financial changes
- Documentation for enterprise workflows and compliance

## Status
- [ ] Recurring payments and expenses defined
- [ ] P&L / cash flow reporting implemented
- [ ] Role-based authorization added
- [ ] Audit logging support built

## Notes
Phase 6 prepares TokBiz for higher transaction volume, automated financial operations, and enterprise-ready workflows.
