# Phase 5: Mobile, Multi-Currency & Channels

## Goal
Extend TokBiz beyond the web dashboard to support mobile-first businesses, multi-currency operations, and integrated local channels for transactions and communication.

## Scope
- Mobile-responsive UI and native mobile app support
- Multi-currency pricing, reporting, and exchange rate handling
- Local payment channel integration for transfers, mobile money, and USSD
- Offline-first workflows and data synchronization
- Business communication channels: WhatsApp, SMS, email notifications
- Digital receipts and supplier/customer communication templates

## Deliverables
- Mobile-friendly and native mobile UX designs
- Frontend support for currency selection and exchange rate management
- Backend support for multi-currency amounts, conversions, and reporting
- APIs for payment channels and transaction reconciliation
- Notification workflows for invoices, payments, and stock alerts
- Documentation of mobile and channel integrations

## Status
- [ ] Mobile/responsive UI defined
- [ ] Multi-currency model support added
- [ ] Payment channel connectors scoped
- [ ] Offline sync and cache strategy planned
- [ ] Notifications and receipts workflows implemented

## Notes
Phase 5 bridges the product from desktop-first operations to real-world MSME adoption, especially for mobile and cash-based commerce.
