# Phase 8: Commercialization, Global Expansion & Platform Maturity

## Goal
Turn TokBiz into a commercial-grade platform with global reach, recurring revenue models, enterprise-grade compliance, and a mature partner ecosystem.

## Scope
- Launch subscription and transaction-based monetization for businesses and service partners
- Support multi-region operations, cross-border payments, and FX-aware settlement
- Build partner marketplaces, revenue sharing, commission tracking, and partner governance
- Add premium AI and analytics products for forecasting, pricing optimization, and growth planning
- Harden security, compliance, SLA, uptime, and operational monitoring

## Deliverables
- Monetization plan with subscription tiers, transaction fees, and partner commission flows
- Multi-region, multi-currency settlement and cross-border payment support
- Partner lifecycle management with onboarding, trust scoring, and dispute handling
- Premium AI/analytics product offerings and usage tracking
- Compliance dashboard, audit reporting, and production operations documentation

## Status
- [ ] Commercial pricing and tier strategy defined
- [ ] Global expansion and cross-border settlement scoped
- [ ] Premium analytics and AI products modeled
- [ ] Partner revenue sharing and compliance workflows documented

## Notes
Phase 8 makes TokBiz a mature SaaS marketplace platform ready for external customers, revenue growth, and regulated enterprise operations.
